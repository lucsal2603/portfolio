05 / Listening

A speaker should disappear before the music does.

AETHER 01 is the result of seven prototypes and twenty-two years of arguments about what a reference monitor should sound like. The answer, in the end, was almost nothing. No tilt, no flavour, no character introduced by the cabinet — only the recording, returned faithfully.

To get there we removed everything that could move when it shouldn't. The chassis is milled from a single titanium billet, the driver suspended on a magnesium yoke, the internal chamber lined with hand-felted wool from a single Sardinian flock. Nothing was painted. Nothing was added. Everything earns its place.

The first owner heard it in our atelier in February. She listened for nineteen minutes, ordered two, and asked us not to change a thing. We haven't.

— G. Marenzi, Founder Milano · Studio · 2026
